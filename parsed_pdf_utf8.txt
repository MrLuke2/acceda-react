  Elevator  Pitch 
 
ACCEDA  is  an  AI-powered  accessibility  platform  that  scans,  fixes,  and  verifies  WCAG  compliance  automatically  —  helping  developers  ship  accessible  software  with  confidence.

  Brand  Voice 
 
Clear Communicate  without  jargon.  Every  message  should  be  immediately  understood. 
Technical Respect  the  developer  audience.  Use  precise  language  and  real  examples. 
Trustworthy Every  claim  is  backed  by  evidence.  Compliance  is  not  guesswork. 
Helpful Meet  users  where  they  are.  Provide  actionable  guidance,  not  just  reports.
